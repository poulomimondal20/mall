Why This Property

Where 40 million
people choose to go.

Located 10 minutes from Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport, MOA sits at the center of a 22-state trade area. Our visitors don't just shop — they stay, dine, play, and return. Average dwell time exceeds 3 hours, spending per visit outpaces every comparable property in North America.

  • Regional Gravity — 22-state primary trade area. Accessible from 38 states within a 2-hour flight.
  • Year-Round Traffic — No outdoor exposure. 365-day operation with consistent, climate-controlled foot traffic regardless of season.
  • Affluent Demographics — Average household income $85K+. 60% of visitors travel more than 150 miles specifically to visit MOA.
  • Hotel Ecosystem — 6 hotels connected or adjacent to the property, with 6,000+ rooms within 2 miles.
  • Tax-Free Shopping — Minnesota's clothing tax exemption is a national draw. Shoppers choose MOA for the savings.
